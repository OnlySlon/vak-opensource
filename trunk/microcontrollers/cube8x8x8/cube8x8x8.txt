# Cube 8x8x8

Manufactured by www.syyyd.com.

Pin | Signal | Function
----|--------|----------
 1  | VCC    |
 2  | Y0     | Layer select, bit 0
 3  | Y1     | Layer select, bit 1
 4  | Y2     | Layer select, bit 2
 5  | Y3     | Control LED1...LED4
 6  | Y4     | Control LED5...LED8
 7  | SDI    | Serial data   \
 8  | CLK    | Clock         | to shift registers
 9  | /LE    | Latch enable  |
 10 | /OE    | Output enable /
 11 | EXT    | LED enable
 12 | GND    |
